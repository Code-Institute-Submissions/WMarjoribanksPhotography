A bit about me...

My name is William Marjoribanks and the photos you see here, were all taken by me. I began, as all photographers do, an amateur enthusiast. I read every book I could and tuned in to all the YouTube tutorials my data-limits would allow. After that, it was a case of getting out there, into the world, to capture the split second which will determine the difference between memorable and forgotten. I've travelled to over 30 countries in the search for my perfect photo. Some posed photos, some candid. Regardless, the search continues, and some of my work is I hope, here for you to enjoy.

Supporting Your Local Artist

The freedom of being able to work on photography projects for me, is one of passion, and isn't something I take for granted. If you're in the position to be able to support a local artist, I implore you to do so. It isn't solely for financial gain, but more so that society can learn about the larger world and the people who inhabit it. Sharing culture is of paramount importance. That's what my aim is. If you see a piece in my collection which speaks to you, I use PicFair for print production. They use local printers to your nearest city, with market-leading equipment ensuring high quality products.
