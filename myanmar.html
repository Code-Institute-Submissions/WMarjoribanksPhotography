Myanmar

Travelling to Myanmar had been a lifelong dream, fulfilled with everything I could expect and more. The sheer beauty integrated amongst it's cultural backstory with Buddhism at its centre, wasn't something I could have ever been prepared for. I was fortunate to have been able to explore Myanmar, for one month. An afternoon arrival introduced me to bustling downtown Yangon, blinded by the glimmer of Sule Pagoda and a whirlwind of streetfood vendors hard at work, the friendly echoes of "Mingalaba", translation "auspiciousness upon you", had me surrounded by a society built upon Thai, Indian and Chinese influences, wanting only to show the best of what their country has in store.
