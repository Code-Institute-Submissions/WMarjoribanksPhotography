Malaysia

Malaysia, with similarities to my previous travel in Myanmar, is a melting pot of nationalities and cultures. Malaysia is a predominantly Muslim country, however when I arrived in February 2019, I was just in time to witness Thaipusam.
Thaipusam is celebrated largely by the Tamil community, which requires strict discipline with regards to religious responsibilities, diet in preparation for a final public ceremony. This ceremony concludes at the peak of 272 steps leading into Batu Caves.
